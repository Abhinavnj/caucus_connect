What is Caucus Connect?

Caucus Connect is a service that was created to serve the needs of any and all Secaucus High School students in their search for relevant information regarding their future career/educational endeavors. By matching prospective users with fellow SHS alumni, in the relevant career fields and educational institutions, our service seeks to keep SHS connected from top to bottom; we accomplish this by connecting students with the information they need to succeed, while allowing alumni to foster the growth of students that may one day become valuable contributors to their own industry.
